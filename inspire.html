Nadeshot

Matthew "Nadeshot" Hagg is a retired Professional Call Of Duty that played for Optic Gaming from 2008-2015. In this time Mr. Hagg went from a average teen working at his local McDonalds to becoming one of the top players in video game history. With Nadeshot winning Gold at the 2014 X Games to winning the best eSports player of the year in 2014, he paved the way for the next generation of eSports atheletes.

Nadeshot in now currently the owner of his very own eSports orginaztion known as 100 Thieves based in California.
